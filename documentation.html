Platform Overview

What is Ayurveda Trace?

Ayurveda Trace is a blockchain-powered platform that ensures complete transparency and traceability in the Ayurvedic herb supply chain. From harvest to final product, every step is recorded on an immutable blockchain ledger.

Our platform connects all stakeholders in the supply chain - from herb collectors to final packagers - creating a trusted ecosystem where quality and authenticity are guaranteed.

Key Benefits

  • • Complete supply chain transparency
  • • Immutable quality records
  • • Authentic product verification
  • • Regulatory compliance
  • • Consumer trust building

Getting Started

Step 1: Connect Your Wallet

To interact with the blockchain, you need to connect a compatible wallet like MetaMask.

Wallet Connection Process

  1. 1 Install MetaMask browser extension
  2. 2 Click "Connect Wallet" button in the header
  3. 3 Approve the connection request in MetaMask
  4. 4 Verify connection status in the header

Step 2: Choose Your Role

Select your role in the supply chain to access relevant features and functions.

Available Roles

  • • Collector: Gathers raw herbs
  • • Processor: Processes raw materials
  • • Tester: Quality control testing
  • • Packager: Final product packaging
  • • Admin: System management

Demo Mode

Don't have a wallet? Use demo mode to explore the platform without blockchain connection.

Step 3: Start Using the Platform

Once connected and authenticated, you can access all features relevant to your role.

User Roles & Responsibilities

Collector

Responsible for gathering raw herbs from natural sources and recording initial collection data.

Key Functions:

  • • Record herb collection location
  • • Document collection time and date
  • • Upload photos of collected herbs
  • • Generate initial batch QR codes
  • • Ensure sustainable harvesting practices

Processor

Handles the initial processing and preparation of raw herbs for further manufacturing.

Key Functions:

  • • Clean and sort raw herbs
  • • Record processing methods
  • • Document processing parameters
  • • Update batch status on blockchain
  • • Maintain quality standards

Tester

Conducts quality control testing and analysis to ensure herb purity and potency.

Key Functions:

  • • Perform quality analysis tests
  • • Document test results
  • • Verify herb authenticity
  • • Upload lab reports and certificates
  • • Ensure compliance with standards

Packager

Responsible for final product packaging and preparing for distribution to consumers.

Key Functions:

  • • Package final products
  • • Generate product QR codes
  • • Record packaging details
  • • Update final product status
  • • Ensure proper labeling

Admin

Manages the overall system, user accounts, and ensures platform integrity.

Key Functions:

  • • Manage user accounts and roles
  • • Monitor system performance
  • • Generate system reports
  • • Handle dispute resolution
  • • Ensure data integrity

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I connect my wallet?

Click the "Connect Wallet" button in the header, install MetaMask if you haven't already, and approve the connection request. Your wallet address will appear in the header once connected.

Can I use the platform without a wallet?

Yes! Use the demo mode to explore all features without connecting a wallet. Demo mode provides sample data to demonstrate the platform's capabilities.

How secure is my data?

All data is stored on the blockchain, making it immutable and tamper-proof. Your personal information is encrypted and only accessible to authorized parties.

How do I verify product authenticity?

Simply scan the QR code on any Ayurveda Trace product using our platform or any QR scanner. You'll see the complete journey of the product from collection to packaging.
